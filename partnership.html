General FAQ

SkyJumper assigns a designated party host to your birthday party who will ensure everything runs smoothly. We've got it all covered, from creating the invite video to set up a special birthday party space equipped for a food buffet and cake ceremony.

You can choose between 2 and 3 hours of total activity time. 45 minutes are set aside for food and cake cutting; the rest is activity time.

We recommend booking your party at least 15 days in advance to ensure availability.

Birthday parties can be rescheduled or cancelled up to 7 days before the event date for a full refund. Cancellations made within 7 days of the event are subject to a cancellation fee.

You can book a birthday party through by filling the form on our website by clicking here or by calling us directly on +91 8882288001

To confirm your event, a deposit is required to secure at the event. The remaining balance is due on the day of the event.

We ask for a minimum of 10 guests to book an event and can accommodate upwards of a maximum of 300 guests when you rent out the park exclusively!
